ABOUT THE MOCKTAILS

Mocktails are a non-alcoholic beverage, the opposite of cocktails. Though that most definitely does not mean it is not a fun, delicious, and stylish drink. The number of ingredients you can add into your mocktail are endless! Besides the alcohol of course. Take a break from the liquor, see what fabulous drinks you can come up with no matter the day, month, and year. A mocktail is the perfect drink for any time. Take a look through the website and see four drinks that are the best match depending on each of the four seasons: winter, spring, summer, and fall! Each season throughout the year has the ability to make an individual feel a certain way. For instance, winter being cozy and warm, or summer being sunny and hot! It is safe to say all four seasons bring there own comfort. Which is why this website provides the best mocktail for the seaons. Give them a try to find out which one of the four will be your favourite!

FOUR DRINKS FOUR SEASONS

The four mocktail recipes provided are the perfect drinks for each season. Winter Blues is the ever so easy yet delicious blueberry mocktail that will keep you refreshed, and help you wind down on a december evening. Next up we have spring! The flowers and plants are blooming, the best way to welcome spring would be with the Berry Bubbly. This mocktail will soon be your everyday craving with the slight fizz and sweet strawberry puree mix. Let's move onto the summer season. The sun is bright and the pool is cool, the mocktail in your hand is the Sweet Beach. This tropical mocktail gives a burst of sweet yet tangy flavours all at once. Finally, the fall season is making its way towards us. To say goodbye to the green leaves, let's make Minty Zest. The mocktail is full of lime and full of bubbly goodness. Take a look above for each season's mocktail recipe!
