BERRY BUBBLY:

RECIPE

There is no such thing as too many strawberries. Here is a link to a strawberry mojito mocktail!

INGREDIENTS:

    - Strawberries: 1 ½
    - 1 lime
    - 10 mint leaves
    - Sweetener of choice: ½ cup
    - 4 cups of Sprite
    - Ice cubes

DIRECTIONS:

    1. In a large jug add your strawberries, lime juice, and mint leaves including the sweetener.
    2. Mash all of these ingredients with a muddler, or anything to help you mash all the ingredients together.
    3. Strain this mixture to take out the juice.
    4. Pick your pitcher of choice. Add in your ice cubes and add the sprite. Slowly begin pouring the strained juice into the
    pitcher and give it a quick mix.

SERVE:

Finish it off by garnishing with more strawberries. Sip on Berry Bubbly as the spring season begins to bloom!
