WINTER BLUES:

RECIPE

Loving the blueberries? Click here to check out another simple blueberry mocktail!

INGREDIENTS:

    - Blueberries: 1 Â½ cups
    - 4 cups of water
    - 4 tbsp powdered sugar
    - 5 Cups lemon lime seltzer Seltzer
    - Ice cubes
    - Fresh Mint leaves

DIRECTIONS:

    1. Place water and blueberries in a pot and let it boil on medium heat.
    2. Once it begins boiling, add your powdered sugar to the boiling mixture and let it dissolve.
    3. Lower the heat in order to bring the mixture to a simmer.
    4. Use the back of a mixing spoon to smash the blueberries while they simmer.
    5. Let it simmer for about 10 minutes. Then remove the pot from the stove and set it aside to cool.
    6. Once cooled, begin straining the mixture. Get out as much liquid as possible.
    7. Place liquid mixture into a glass and add your ice. Top it off with your lemon lime seltzer.

SERVE:

Garnish with mint leaves and add in a few blueberries. Enjoy Winter Blues with your friends and family during the festive season and holidays!
