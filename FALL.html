MINTY ZEST:

RECIPE

Refreshing drinks always hit the spot. If you agree, this cucumber and lime mocktail is also perfect for you!

INGREDIENTS:

    - Mint leaves: 6-7 leaves
    - Grated ginger: ½ teaspoon
    - Grated cucumber: 1 tablespoon
    - Juice of half a lime
    - 5 cups of sparkling water
    - 3 cups of Ginger Ale

DIRECTIONS:

    1. Muddle your ginger, cucumber, and add the lime juice in a bowl.
    2. Strain this mixture and set aside the liquid. Extract all the juice from ginger and cucumber.
    3. Add mint leaves to the bottom of the pitcher.
    4. Cut the lime into thin slices and add to the pitcher.
    5. Add your ice cubes and pour the Ginger Ale and sparkling water into the pitcher.
    6. Stir the mix.

SERVE:

Cut your fruit of choice (or the ones preferred above) and garnish your drink with them. Top it off with some coconut shavings. Drink “sweet beach” while you breathe in the ocean air and feel the sand beneath your feet.
